EmpireZ Event

The Hunt for Luke

Treasure hunt / scavenger hunt — admin setup guide and full clue chain for players.

Admin / Pre-Event Notes

This event requires day-of setup and PBO work from Zerk so custom lootable items can be placed with custom clue descriptions. If ROI isn’t worth the effort, skip it — but if you run it, follow the steps below in order.

  • Configure each clue as a lootable item that instantly respawns when picked up (not a permanent static prop players cannot take).
  • Instant respawn lets multiple players grab the same clue at once without waiting on admins.
  • Place items exactly as noted in each step (yellow-arrow screenshots are references). Decorative props (house asset, carbonite display, etc.) can stay non-lootable.
  • Set up a turn-in point at the Jedi Temple or west-side Jedi structure — players must submit all 12 clue items to win.

Event Mechanic — Collect & Turn In

Players follow clues in order, but must physically loot and keep every clue item along the way. At the finale they turn in the full set — no hand-holding required at each step, and no skipping ahead without proof of completion.

12 clue items to collect:

  1. Power Converter
  2. Moisture Evaporator
  3. R2 Unit
  4. Obi-Wan Lightsaber
  5. Falcon Datapad
  6. Pilot Helmet
  7. Vader Mask
  8. Han in Carbonite
  9. Mechanical Hand
  10. Boushh Helm
  11. Palpatine’s Saber
  12. Yoda Force Ghost
Day of event — player announcement: The treasure hunt has started! Begin at Tosche Station.

How to play: Find each clue item, inspect it (tab the item → middle mouse button) to read where to go next, then pick it up and keep it. Items respawn instantly for other players.

To win: Turn in all 12 clue items at the Jedi Temple (or west-side Jedi structure) when you reach the end.

★

Finale — Rewards

Setup: At the Jedi Temple or the massive Jedi structure on the west side of the map, set up a turn-in point (crate, vendor, or admin hand-in).

  • Players must submit all 12 clue items to complete the hunt and receive rewards.
  • No partial turn-ins — the full set proves they followed the chain without skipping steps.
  • Because clue spawns instantly respawn, admins do not need to supervise each location.

Player: Turn in Power Converter, Moisture Evaporator, R2 Unit, Obi-Wan Lightsaber, Falcon Datapad, Pilot Helmet, Vader Mask, Han in Carbonite, Mechanical Hand, Boushh Helm, Palpatine’s Saber, and Yoda Force Ghost → collect your reward.

Congratulations — the Hunt for Luke is complete.
