EmpireZ Event

The Hunt for Luke

Treasure hunt / scavenger hunt — admin setup guide and full clue chain for players.

Admin / Pre-Event Notes

This event requires day-of setup and PBO work from Zerk so custom objects can be placed with custom clue descriptions. If ROI isn’t worth the effort, skip it — but if you run it, follow the steps below in order.

  • Prepare all static items, NPCs, and custom descriptions before go-live.
  • Hide or place objects exactly as noted in each step (yellow-arrow screenshots are references).
  • Final rewards go at the Jedi Temple or the large Jedi structure on the west side of the map.
Day of event — player announcement: The treasure hunt has started! Begin at Tosche Station.

Important: Players must inspect event items to read clue descriptions — tab to the item, then click middle mouse button.
1

Tosche Station — Droid Factory

Setup: Hide a static power converter between the two droids on the top level of the droid factory.

Clue on power converterI never made it off this shelf, Though once I dreamed of more myself. A restless farmboy came to call, But chores would always stall it all. If moisture’s what you seek to find, Go where twin suns scorch and blind. There ‘neath domes of humble clay, His family watched him drift away.

→ Leads players to the Lars Homestead

2

Lars Homestead

Setup:

  • Place the Lars house asset near the admin shop.
  • Add a moisture evaporator and a static Jawa.
  • Jawa inspect description contains the next clue.
Clue on JawaTraded good, traded fast — you lookin’ for the shiny one? Not here, not now... he’s rollin’ with the rustin’ ton. We sold, we moved — left tracks in sand so wide. Big crawl, big haul — metal hills that glide. Look where scrappers sleep and oil runs deep That’s where your next secret’ll beep.

→ Leads players to the Sandcrawler

3

Sandcrawler

Setup: Place a static R2 unit at the sandcrawler for players to inspect.

Clue on R2The boy’s path turned when suns were high, Past dunes where metal bones still lie. He followed one who knew the way, Toward stone and shadowed hideaways. No farms, no kin — just watchful eyes, And towers poking at the skies. Find the place where names mean naught, And all that’s lost is sold or bought.

→ Leads players to Mos Eisley Cantina

4

Mos Eisley Cantina

Setup: Hide an Obi-Wan lightsaber behind the table behind the bar in the cantina.

Clue on lightsaberSwift was the cut that cleared our way, A flash of light, then no delay. What words could not, coin swiftly spoke — And wheels of fate began to smoke. Now leave this den of wary eyes, For where escape through engines lies. Your passage waits beneath the sun, Its hum and hull a loaded gun.

→ Leads players to the Millennium Falcon at Mos Eisley

5

Millennium Falcon — Mos Eisley

Setup: Hide a datapad near the Falcon.

Clue on datapadA moon that burned, a star undone, One shot fired, and darkness spun. A farmboy’s aim, a smuggler’s hand, Together turned the tide to sand. But triumph paints a target wide — Now hunted, scattered, forced to hide. Seek the silence, buried deep, Where breath turns fog and steel must sleep. There waits the frost, the rebel flame, A base unknown, without a name.

→ Leads players to Hoth

6

Hoth

Beyond the maps, beyond the light, A world of shadow, sense, and fright. Where roots entwine and visions wait, And patience carves the hands of fate

7

Yoda’s Hut — Dagobah

Cloud City

  • Place Han in carbonite next to the parked Slave I.
  • Place a mechanical Hand item on the ground somewhere beneath Cloud City (Luke’s severed hand).
Clue on Han in carboniteOne was taken, sealed in stone, Another lost, now lies alone. A prize was claimed and flown away, But what was severed chose to stay. Look not to stars, but far below — Where silent scraps no longer glow. Beneath the place where shadows land, You'll find the fate of a fallen hand
Clue on mechanical handThe blade is gone, the truth was told, A name revealed, a grip grown cold. Friends are scattered, hope is thin — But shadowed halls still hold our kin. No throne of light, no crown of grace, Just coils and chains in a desolate place. To sand and stone our path now bends, Where fear begins… and rescue ends

Jabba’s Palace

10

Death Star

Ewok Village

Finale — Rewards
